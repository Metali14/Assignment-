ABOUT US

We specialize in making exceptional content for education and any career-related videos. We are an emerging platform on YouTube for content creators. We strive to present our viewers not only with in-depth knowledge of engineering principles but also with the interesting and latest information about technology, careers, and more. Our approach is not just like any other platform. We want to provide knowledge not just in theory, but also explain it with practical scenarios. We acknowledge the fact that college courses mostly just skim the surface in one or two semesters, and paid courses online are one of the only ways to get detailed practical knowledge in any field. So we, at Code Wrestling, want to bring knowledge on an accessible platform and display it interestingly and straightforwardly, while preserving as much detail as possible.
